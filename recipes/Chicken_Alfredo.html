Chicken_Alfredo

Description

Cook chicken breast and toss with fettuccine pasta in a creamy Alfredo sauce made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan. The result is a rich, comforting dish that's sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta (12 oz or 340g)
  • Chicken breasts (2, boneless and skinless, cut into strips)
  • Heavy cream (1 cup)
  • Butter (1/4 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup, grated)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package directions and drain.
  2. In a pan, cook chicken with olive oil until browned and cooked through, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter, add garlic, and sauté briefly. Pour in heavy cream and simmer. Stir in Parmesan until smooth.
  4. Toss the pasta and chicken in the Alfredo sauce. Season with salt, pepper, and garnish with parsley if desired.
