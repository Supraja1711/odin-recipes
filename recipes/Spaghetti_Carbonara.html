Spaghetti_Carbonara

Description

Cook spaghetti and toss it in a creamy sauce made with eggs, Parmesan, pancetta (or bacon), and black pepper. The heat from the pasta creates a velvety sauce that coats each strand. Serve immediately for a rich, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (12 oz or 340g)
  • Pancetta or bacon (4 oz or 115g, chopped)
  • Eggs (3 large, beaten)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup, grated)
  • Fresh black pepper (to taste)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain, reserving some pasta water.
  2. In a pan, cook pancetta (or bacon) until crispy, then add garlic and sauté briefly.
  3. Mix eggs and Parmesan, then toss with hot pasta, adding pancetta and reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  4. Season with black pepper and salt, then serve immediately.
