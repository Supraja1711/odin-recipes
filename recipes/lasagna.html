Lasagna

Description

Cook lasagna noodles, then layer them with ricotta cheese, marinara sauce, mozzarella, and cooked ground beef. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese on top. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (12-15 sheets)
  • Ricotta cheese (15 oz or about 425g)
  • Mozzarella cheese (2 cups shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup grated)
  • Ground beef (1 lb or 450g)
  • Marinara sauce (3-4 cups)
  • Egg (1 large, for mixing with ricotta)
  • Garlic (2-3 cloves, minced)
  • Onion (1, chopped)
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Italian seasoning or basil (optional, for flavor)

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles and prepare the sauce by sautéing onion, garlic, and ground beef, then add marinara.
  2. Mix ricotta with egg and seasoning; layer noodles, ricotta, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  3. Repeat layers, finish with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and lightly golden.
